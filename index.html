About Us

Complete Solutions was formed for the sole purpose of using their Innovative Modular Construction Technology in the design and build process of residential, educational and commercial facilities in Fiji.

After seeing the destruction and damage caused by Cyclone Winston, the company embarked on a mid to long term plan to offer modular building solutions through the Government’s Rehabilitation Programme for Design and Build facilities in 2016 throughout Fiji.

The Modular Methodology has various advantages including speed of construction, durability, cost effectiveness, 10% bigger space than specified while remaining within the stated cost structure, less reliance on local construction material supply, transportability, cost effective quality, quality assurance and new technology

Complete Solutions proposes to use a modular solution for this project and successfully deliver within four to five months once a confirmed order is made.

The majority of design work for this product has already been completed which will provide a significant advantage for the early commencement of construction. Materials used for internal linings are innovative, durable and functional, providing significant advantages over traditional materials used in conventional construction.

The China supplier to Complete Solutions, is a leader in prefab homes and light steel construction and has the capacity and resources to deliver the modular buildings within a tight timeframe.

These will be delivered ready for assembly, with all of the building materials required packed and shipped in the containers from China. Where necessary we can engage our strategic engineering partners, to ensure structures satisfy geo-tech and building code standards. Having already undertaken many schools and building projects abroad, Complete Solutions has the know-how and refined the critical thinking pathway to deliver exactly what you’re after. We also have local Fijian construction contractors, and will undertake demolition, foundation work and the assembly of the buildings on site if necessary to ensure your home is safe and livable.

Complete Solutions and its partners visited some of damaged sites including Teidamu and Koronubu and understand the havoc these natural disasters can cause.

After engaging with the affected local stakeholders in different locations, we view the construction and delivery as time critical and the only solution especially for a tropical destination like Fiji.

Furthermore, these visits confirmed the need for a long term robust solution delivered in a cost effective framework.

Complete Solutions is confident in their proposed Modular Methodology to deliver the needed outcomes.
